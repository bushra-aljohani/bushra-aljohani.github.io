I am an AI researcher and engineer passionate about building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. My work focuses on large language models (LLMs), retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), multi-agent systems, AI for software engineering, and trustworthy AI.

I have authored multiple peer-reviewed publications in applied artificial intelligence, covering topics including vulnerability detection, medical question answering, code quality analysis, and Arabic handwriting recognition. My current research explores agentic AI systems that combine reasoning, retrieval, and verification to improve the reliability of LLM-based applications.

Beyond research, I enjoy transforming academic ideas into practical systems, designing AI architectures, experimenting with emerging models, and building solutions that bridge the gap between research and industry.
